HEBMIC AI

An interactive AI companion for the methodology

HEBMIC AI is being developed as an intelligent companion to the book and framework. Its purpose is to help leaders, boards, governance participants, and strategy teams engage the methodology in a more interactive and applied way.

Access and launch updatesAsk about access

What it will help users do

Explore the logic of the HEBMIC framework, think through institutional challenges, and translate complex strategy questions into a more structured innovation process.

Framework guidanceStrategic explorationInstitutional use cases

Who it is for

Presidents, provosts, trustees, deans, faculty governance leaders, and senior administrators who need a practical way to engage business model innovation without losing legitimacy or mission alignment.
